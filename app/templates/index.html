Project Summary

For my second capstone project during MSDS 696, I built a website using Flask, CouchDB and Python to generate and display visualizations from US Mortgage data sourced from the Consumer Protection Finance Bureau. This mortgage data is submitted to the US Government by US lenders to measure their lending activity, demographics, and loan contracts. I was able to scrape this historical data and load it into a CouchDB database. Afterwards, I built visualizations and implemented an MVVM with flask to parameterize and visualize this data. I used Matplotlib for time series data and I used Folium for the geographical data.

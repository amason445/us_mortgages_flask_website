Financial Metric Metric Definition
Mortgage Loan Volume This is the loan volume for {{ state_long_name }} every year. It measures the number of loans originated within the state.
Mortgage Loan Volume ($) This is the total dollar amount loaned in {{ state_long_name }} every year.
Average Mortgage Rates This is the average interest rates for mortgages in {{ state_long_name }} every year.
Mortgage Loan to Value This is the average loan-to-value ratio for mortgages in {{ state_long_name }} every year. Loan-to-Value is calculated as the loan amount divided by the value of the property, and a larger ratio will indicate a smaller down payment.
